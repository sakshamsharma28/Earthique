Logistics & Eco-Shipping

Shipping Policy

Learn about our eco-friendly packaging, carbon-neutral shipping structures, and transit timelines.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping

Earthique is committed to environmental stewardship. We calculate the carbon output of every delivery package and purchase offsets to fund reforestation initiatives. Our shipping boxes are strictly 100% recyclable, printed with organic soy ink on FSC paper.

Shipping Charges

Delivery rates are calculated based on your total order subtotal:

  • Orders Above ₹999: Free Standard Shipping.
  • Orders Below ₹999: Flat Standard Shipping Charge of ₹99.

Delivery Timelines

We process and hand packages to our cargo partners within 24 hours of purchase. Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Metropolitan Regions (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru): 2 to 3 Business Days.
  • Tier 2 Cities: 3 to 5 Business Days.
  • Remote/Regional Locations: 5 to 7 Business Days.

Please note: Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are processed the following business day.

Loss & Transit Damage

All Earthique cargo is fully insured during transit. In the rare case that your botanical bottle arrives broken, please email a photo to care@earthique.com within 48 hours of delivery, and we will send a replacement immediately.
