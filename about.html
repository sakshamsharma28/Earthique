Our Mission

Earthique was established with a singular focus: to create clean, high-performance skincare that bridges the gap between pure herbal wisdom and dermatological research. We believe that nature holds the answers to healthy, radiant skin. Our mission is to extract those secrets sustainably and deliver them in clean, luxurious, science-backed formulas that protect your skin barrier and respect the Earth.

Our Vision

We envision a beauty industry free from synthetic chemicals, plastic waste, and hollow claims. Earthique aims to lead the clean beauty revolution by proving that natural, vegan, and organic ingredients can perform at clinical levels. We strive to empower men and women to embrace their natural skin health with confidence, clarity, and trust.

Ingredient Philosophy

Every extract in our laboratory undergoes rigorous testing for purity, chemical potency, and skin compatibility. We adhere to strict formulation guidelines:

  • Pure Actives: We source stable bio-actives, like cold-pressed Rosehip, Babchi seeds (Bakuchiol), and Kakadu Plums (stable Vitamin C).
  • Zero Toxins: 100% free from parabens, sulfates, silicones, phthalates, synthetic dyes, and artificial fragrances.
  • Hypoallergenic Bases: Suspending active botanicals in hydrating aloe juice and coconut water, rather than synthetic oils or alcohol.

Sustainability Commitments

We believe clean beauty extends beyond the product inside the container. We run an eco-conscious supply chain focused on reducing carbon waste:

  • Recyclable Glass: Over 90% of our products are packed in custom recyclable amber glass to preserve active chemicals naturally.
  • FSC Cartons: Outer secondary packing utilizes FSC-certified paper, printed with organic soy ink.
  • Ethical Harvesting: We support local organic growers, ensuring crops are harvested sustainably without disrupting regional biodiversity.
