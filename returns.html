Returns & Refunds

Read our 30-day return policies and refund structures designed to guarantee absolute skincare satisfaction.

30-Day Skincare Guarantee

Your skin health is our priority. If a botanical compound does not fit your skin type or concerns, we offer a full refund or exchange within 30 days of purchase. We want you to browse and test our collections with complete peace of mind.

How to Return a Product

To initiate a returns pick-up, please follow these steps:

  1. Email our care team at care@earthique.com containing your order number (e.g. EQ-123456) and the reasons for returning.
  2. Our cargo agents will schedule a free pick-up from your home address within 48 hours.
  3. Pack the bottle safely in its original outer shipping box. Ensure at least 50% of the product volume remains inside the container.

Refund Settlements

Once we receive the returned item at our sanctuary, our pathologists will verify its weight and volume. Approved refunds are processed immediately. The credits will appear in your original bank account or card within 5 to 7 business days, depending on bank transfer speeds.

Exchanges & Replacements

If you wish to exchange a formula for a different concern (e.g. trading an Oily Skin Niacinamide Serum for a Dry Skin Hydrating Moisturizer), please specify this in your email care request, and we will send the replacement package free of extra shipping charges.
